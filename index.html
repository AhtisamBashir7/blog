Use of Humor in Serious Mental Illness


Humor is known to be the major road block in development of anxiety and depression in an individual. The ability to find humor during stress enables a person to refrain from feeling anxious or dwelling in mental health issues.
Humor in serious mental health illness can prevent the loss of hope or suicidal thought in an individual. Humor makes up for the tough parts of life and makes the person strong enough to cope with mental health issues. Most importantly humor enables a person with mental health issues to identify their issues better as they are hyper aware of their conscious and their surrounding.

To have a good sense of humor has numerous long and short term mental health benefits. Short-term mental health benefits of humor includes laughing automatically triggers positive physical and mental changes that help to relax your mind, organ stimulation, stress relief and relief from tension. Long-term benefits of humor on mental health includes a boost to the immune system, pain relief, positivity and improving mood.

The following tips will help you develop sense of humour:
1. Focus your attention on things that make you happy.
2. Embrace a giving spirit.
3. Be Discerning.
4. Find something funny on social media.
